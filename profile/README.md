# Cairn Gate Labs

**Independent AI systems lab.**

---

The bottleneck in AI adoption is not model capability.

It is the gap between what models can do and what organizations can actually build, trust, and maintain — the workflow selection, data readiness, evaluation discipline, and governance that separates a demo from a working system. The market produces more AI surface area every quarter. It produces almost no systems thinking about how to deploy it responsibly at depth.

Cairn Gate exists to close that gap.

---

## What we build

**Workflow diagnostics**
Map processes, surface automation opportunities, assess data readiness. Identify where AI creates measurable value before committing to a build.

**Memory architecture systems**
Proprietary multi-scale memory for AI agents and workflows — episodic, semantic, and procedural layers with provenance-preserving recall and auditable context reconstruction.

**Evaluation harnesses**
Define what "working" means before deployment. Measure it systematically. Monitor it after.

**Technical advisory**
Architecture reviews and implementation partnerships for founders and operators who need honest answers, not sales pitches.

---

## Who we work with

**Technical operators** building AI-augmented workflows inside mid-market companies — people who have budget, a real problem, and no shortage of vendors telling them to just call an API.

**Founders and technical co-founders** who need honest architecture reviews and capable implementation partners.

**Compliance-constrained organizations** who need AI systems that are auditable and provenance-preserving by design.

---

## What we are not

Not an AI agency — we do not white-label models or deliver generic chatbot builds.

Not a thought-leadership platform — we publish when we have something to say that others can't.

Not a venture studio — we incubate our own work, not other people's startups.
