# Cairn Gate Labs

Independent AI systems lab — building practical tools, proprietary architectures, and advisory systems that help organizations turn AI capability into reliable, auditable workflows.

---

**The bottleneck in AI adoption is not model capability.**

It is the gap between what models can do and what organizations can actually build, trust, and maintain — the workflow selection, data readiness, evaluation discipline, and governance that separates a demo from a working system.

---

## What we build

| | |
|---|---|
| **Workflow diagnostics** | Identify where AI creates measurable value before committing to a build |
| **Memory architecture** | Proprietary multi-scale memory systems for AI agents and workflows |
| **Evaluation harnesses** | Measure what "working" means before deployment, monitor it after |
| **Technical advisory** | Architecture reviews and implementation for founders and operators |

## Who we work with

- Technical operators building AI workflows inside mid-market companies
- Founders and technical co-founders who need honest architecture reviews, not sales pitches
- Organizations with compliance constraints who need AI systems auditable by design

---

## What we are not

Not an AI agency. Not a thought-leadership platform. Not a venture studio.
